This website redesign’s purpose is to streamline the process of scheduling a test drive on a car. The process can be completed within a few clicks and no time at all; removing the factor of having to contact the dealership via phone or email, to then wait for a response. This will allow the user to shop freely without submitting unnecessary contact information to the dealership.

Hypothesis Statement

We believe that redesigning classichonda.com will help users to navigate through the dealer website faster and find what they need by improving homepage design, simplifying and cleaning it. We believe this will increase productivity for users and income for dealership.


User-focused motivation

The process to buy a car seems stressful and he is afraid of being pressured into a vehicle he doesn’t want/need.

Potential Solutions

Homepage

  • Dynamic layout, more exciting to view
  • Less clutter
  • More emphasis using the "Search" field

Inventory Pages

  • Tool Tips while hovering over car specs (product page)
  • Text fields are easy to complete not too invasive
  • Options to choose contact if information is submitted
  • Pretty straight forward form to scedule a test drive (on product page)

Overall Site

  • Making the website all in all easier to use
  • Up to date inventory system to reduce confusion to the customer
