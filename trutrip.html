trutrip

A travel app to track the best deals and keep you safe.

Mobile App Case Study

Role: UX Designer

Overview

We’ve neared the end of the pandemic, COVID-19. That means travelers would like to get back to visiting family and traveling the unknown. During this pandemic, all companies complied with the CDC to make sure that all guests of any sort were met with the peace fo mind that the establishment they were visiting, was safe. Trutrip’s goal is to accomodate to that, giving travelers the best deals for flights, hotels and rental cars, as well display sanitation records and reviews.

Problem Statement

Hotels are known for the lack of sanitization when cleaning rooms after other guests, in result, travelers are looking at other options of stay when traveling as there is no way of knowing where its clean to sleep while away.

Solution

To incorporate user reviews into our travel app, it’ll allow people to rate their stay as well give the company a sanitation rating, allowing other users to know which places are best to visit when traveling.

User Research

Interviews

I launched my research with conducting interview questions to gather qualatative and quanitative data to cover a variety of topics such as most reliable/favorite travel apps, customer reviews overview, pains and gains from the current market of apps, and the logistics going into planning a vacation. After conducting 5 interviews via Zoom, I concluded many users rely on personal reviews from customers; but don’t necessarily leave reviews. As well, many apps present unneccessary deals, prices, and extras that users are not interested with, leaving them with wanting a more simplistic, more staright to the point experience. The age demographic targeted for interviews was 20 to 40 years old.
